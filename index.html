Projects

SearchIT!



Description:

The SearchIT! is a mobile app like Google Lens on Android where you can search a certain thing then it will provide the definition of it via Google. In this application, the user will be capturing an image from the device and provides the description and the use of it. Also, the user can be directed to a certain website to know more about the captured image. The user can also find another related info regarding on the image he/she captured. Additionally, SearchIT! also allows the users to save the captured image to their external storage and they can also share the image through different social media platforms.

OKES Classroom Management System



Description:

The Ocean of Knowledge Grades Management System is a system for keeping track of users, teachers, subjects, and students' information, grades, and attendance. There are three users: the teachers, the students, and the administrator. Each of them has a designated account for the login process.

Users' accounts, teachers' information, and subjects can all be managed by administrators. The teachers have the ability to add new students to their class, as well as grade them and keep track of attendance. The Students can only see their grades and attendance if they try to log in to their accounts.

Movie Ticket System



Description:

This system allows users purchase tickets and add-ons for the movie of their choice. When a person purchases something while signed in, money will be taken out of their virtual wallet and they will receive reward points based on how much they spent on the tickets.The user will be prompted to specify an amount if a transaction is made without using an account. The transaction won't go through if this payment is less than the entire cost of the deal. A receipt for the transaction will also be sent by the system to the specified email address.

The website's administrators have the ability to add, update, and delete movies and add-ons, as well as offer a movie archive option and a graph that shows the number of tickets sold for each movie on the particular day or days.
